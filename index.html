FlipWord Coding Challenge
  • Hitesh Yalamanchili

Instructions

The game will start once you submit your preferences. Your goal is to press the buttons in the right order to turn all buttons off (red state). Each button is associated with other buttons such that whenever a button is pressed, it flips the state of itself and of its associated buttons. To start, the buttons will all be on.

In the form below, you can specify the number of buttons you want to play with and the number of associations you want each button to have (not including itself).

Good luck!
